Futhi The grandchild of UpCyclin

Empowring sustainable fashion

Meaning:Again.

Futhi is an isiZulu term that translates to “again”. This is a fitting name as our company utilizes mostly discarded or pre-loved clothing items to create new and dynamic capsule collections that provide the consumer with variety without having purchased large volumes of clothes. Futhi is what we like to call the love child of sustainable consumption and cutting edge fashion.

Newchoices

Where UpcycLin has created dialogue and asked the hard questions to the consumer about their habits and business about their practices, Futhi acts as a den of exploration where the consumer can make new choices about their fashion and clothing consumption.
